"I love programming"

"I grew up in the 90's and spent my childhood in the Koue-bokkeveld and Warme-bokkeveld, South-Africa. I grew up playing outside with bricks and sticks, the bricks was my cars and busses, the sticks was my horsies and this was all imagination play.

Computers was the new thing on the block, it was so new that only a few had access to computers and internet dail-up connections. I believe that persons who grew up with imaginative play leaves a lot of room for a whole load of innovation and imagination.

Because of this reason, I believe that people who grew up like this have an innate ability to use their imagination for all sorts of things.

Imagination is all you need to be a software engineer, dedication of course along with discipline and a will to learn and sheer resilience.

But why would you want to listen to me ?
I have been a software engineer for over a decade, but that is not the ability I have to make you soak up code like a sponge.
The ability I have is to take concepts and break them down and explain it so that it is easy to understand."
